The RUMDUL Story

Where tradition meets modern coffee culture with a variety of brews from around the world

From Humble Beginnings

Established 2018

Rumdul Coffee was born from a simple vision: to bring authentic Vietnamese coffee, Italian espresso, and a variety of international blends to Phnom Penh while creating a modern space that resonates with today's generation.

Named after Cambodia's national flower, the Rumdul represents purity and beauty - values we infuse into every cup we serve.

What started as a small family dream has grown into a beloved community hub where tradition meets innovation.

Our Philosophy

Quality First

We source premium beans from Vietnam and around the world, along with fresh ingredients for every drink.

Tradition Respected

Authentic phin filter brewing methods preserved from generations.

Innovation Embraced

Creative twists on classics for the modern coffee enthusiast.

Community Focused

A space that brings people together over great coffee.
